Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Prep time: 10 mins

Yield: 4

Ingredients





Steps

  1. Strip together butter and parmesan cheese in a small bowl.
  2. Spread 1 1/2 tsp. butter mixture on 1 side of each bread slice. place 4 bread slice, buttered sides down, on wax paper. Top with provolone and mozzarellea cheese, top with remaining bread slices; buttered sides up.
  3. Cook sandwiches in batcher,on a hot griddle or in a a non-stick skillet over medium heat, gently pressing with a spatula, 4minutes on each side or until golden brown and cheese is melted.
